About Us

Masville National High School Senior High School is a public educational institution located in Masville Paranaque City. It offers various academic tracks for senior high school students, including Academic Track, Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track, and Arts and Design Track. The school aims to provide quality education and prepare students for higher education or the workforce. Students can choose specialized subjects based on their chosen track to help them develop skills and knowledge in their areas of interest.

Strand And Courses

Masville National High School Senior High School offers a variety of courses within each academic track like in Academic Track, offers (ABM) Accountancy, Business, and Management and (HUMSS) Humanities and Social Sciences. Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track offers (ICT) Information and Communication Technology and (TVL) Home Economics and cookery.
